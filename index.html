PIG 50 GAME

  1. Click on Roll Dice and your Current score will add up to main score if you keep rolling.
  2. Current Score will be lost if you Roll Dice 1.
  3. You can hold your score and let the other player roll dice by clicking on Hold.
  4. Player to score the fastest 50 wins.
  5. Click on New game to play again.
